Fiscal Year Internal Help Desk Operations Plan

Created by: Humjol Maan, Help Desk Supervisor

Reason for the Plan

Hello, my name is Humjol Maan, and I serve as the Help Desk Supervisor. Alongside my role, I am pursuing a Diploma in Computer Systems Technician – Infrastructure and Services at Sheridan College. This Fiscal Year Internal Help Desk Operations Plan outlines our goals, strategies, and performance improvements for the upcoming year.

The plan focuses on enhancing service quality, staff engagement, and customer satisfaction. All objectives are measured quarterly from October 2025 to December 2025 to ensure progress and accountability.

Mission

Our mission is to provide timely, reliable, and professional IT support services to all internal users. We aim to reduce downtime, enhance user experience, and ensure quick resolutions to technical issues. This mission directly benefits users by keeping their workflows smooth, secure, and efficient.

Vision

Our vision is to evolve into a proactive and data-driven help desk that uses analytics, automation, and training to anticipate user needs. By continuously improving, we aim to become a model of IT service excellence within the organization and beyond.

SMART Goals for FY2025–2026

Goal 1: Staff Satisfaction

Objective: Routinely gather and monitor staff satisfaction input. Increase employee satisfaction by 10% — from a baseline of 72% in October 2025 to 82% — through monthly team feedback sessions and quarterly surveys.

Measurement: Measured quarterly beginning in October 2025 and achieving the goal by December 2025.

User Benefit: Higher staff satisfaction leads to more motivated support agents, resulting in improved service quality for users.

Goal 2: Ongoing Customer Satisfaction

Objective: Improve transactional survey results following incident or request handling by raising ongoing satisfaction by 8% — from 80% in October 2025 to 88% — using post-ticket surveys, coaching agents, and tracking scores below 70%.

Measurement: Reviewed monthly starting October 2025 and reaching target by December 2025.

User Benefit: Users receive friendlier and more effective support, ensuring each interaction feels valued and resolved professionally.

Goal 3: Average Time to Respond

Objective: Reduce average response time by 15% — from 5 minutes in October 2025 to 4 minutes and 15 seconds — through callback/chat options, optimized routing, and staffing improvements.

Measurement: Tracked monthly starting in October 2025 and achieving the target by December 2025.

User Benefit: Faster response times reduce frustration and increase overall satisfaction with IT support.

Goal 4: First Contact Resolution Rate

Objective: Increase the First Contact Resolution Rate by 7% — from 55% in October 2025 to 62% — by offering refresher training, improving knowledge base access, and providing better troubleshooting scripts.

Measurement: Monitored quarterly starting October 2025 and achieving the goal by December 2025.

User Benefit: More issues resolved on first contact mean less waiting and a more positive service experience.

Goal 5: Self-Service Effectiveness

Objective: Increase self-service effectiveness by 12% — from 40% in October 2025 to 52% of tickets resolved through self-service — by improving FAQs, updating top 25 articles, and launching an awareness campaign.

Measurement: Tracked quarterly starting October 2025 and reaching target by December 2025.

User Benefit: Users can solve more issues independently, saving time and reducing dependency on live support.
